WHY EVA

Six reasons
drivers don't go back.

EVA — ethylene-vinyl acetate — is a closed-cell foam laminate. It doesn't absorb water, doesn't hold odour, and doesn't tear. We take that material and build it around your floorpan.

01

Waterproof core

Closed-cell foam means liquid sits on top, never soaks in. Rain, slush, coffee — all stay on the mat.

02

Wash in minutes

Pull out, hose down, shake dry. No deep-cleaning, no specialist products, no carpet residue.

03

Odour-free

EVA doesn't trap moisture, so it doesn't grow the smell that ages a car's interior.

04

Anti-slip system

A grip-textured underside plus OEM-style fastenings hold the mat exactly where it should be.

05

Made to measure

Cut from your vehicle's template — every cut-out, edge and tunnel mapped to your floorpan.

06

Your colour, your trim

Twelve mat colours, ten trim colours. Match your interior or stand it apart.

PROCESS

How ordering works

From first message to finished mats in your boot — usually within a week.

01

Tell us your vehicle

Send make, model, year and any trim notes — by form, phone or social. We'll confirm what we hold on file.

02

Choose colours

Pick your mat colour and trim. We'll send a digital preview so you see it before we cut.

03

We measure or cut

If we already hold your vehicle's template we go straight to cutting. If not, drive to Sallins — measuring takes 40 - 60 minutes.

04

Fitted & finished

Collect from the studio or arrange delivery. We fit them on the spot if you wait, and you drive home with new floors.
