Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

EVA Car Mats Ireland is a custom automotive accessories business based in Sallins, Co. Kildare, Ireland. We provide EVA car mats, seat covers, and window tinting services.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at 089 465 5600.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or contact us:

  • Your name and phone number when you submit a quote or contact form
  • Your vehicle details (make, model, year) provided voluntarily for quoting purposes
  • Your preferred contact language
  • Basic usage data collected automatically by the website (page visits, browser type) via standard web analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • Prepare and deliver your order
  • Contact you regarding your vehicle and service preferences

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil your order or respond to your enquiry. We do not store payment card details.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly at 089 465 5600.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to our social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
