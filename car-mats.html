The Material

What is EVA?

EVA — ethylene-vinyl acetate — is a closed-cell foam laminate. The material is hydrophobic, lightweight, dimensionally stable and odour-neutral. It's the same family of foam used in marine, aviation and orthotic applications.

We bond two layers — a textured wear layer over a romb (diamond) base — so the mat absorbs impact, traps debris in the cells, and presents a clean, even surface back to the cabin. No carpet to soak, no rubber to crack, no smell to develop.

Care & Cleaning

Five minutes, twice a year.

That's the entire maintenance schedule. No special products, no annual carpet shampoo, no garage trip.

  1. step 01
    Pull out, give a shake. Let crumbs and grit fall away.
  2. step 02
    Rinse with a hose. Soap if needed. No pressure washer required.
  3. step 03
    Air-dry in a few minutes. Lay flat back into the footwell.
