The Craft

Not generic.
Not one-size-fits-all.
Yours.

EVA Car Mats Ireland now offers custom-made seat covers, individually tailored for each vehicle. Every cover is sewn to match your car's exact seat shape — with your choice of material, colour, and stitching.

We don't sell universal seat covers off a shelf. We measure your vehicle's specific seat profile, cut to your dimensions, and hand-finish each set before it leaves the studio. The result is a flush, factory-quality fit that protects your original upholstery and transforms your interior.

Materials

Three finishes.
One standard of quality.

Each material is selected for a different lifestyle and use case. Not sure which? We'll advise in person at the studio.

001 / ECO Leather

ECO Leather

Sleek, durable, and easy to wipe clean. The practical choice for taxi drivers, family cars, and anyone who needs a surface that forgives daily life. Available in black, grey, and dark brown.

002 / Alcantara

Alcantara Fabric

Soft-touch, premium feel. Brings a sportier, more refined look to any interior. Ideal if you want something that looks and feels different from standard leather alternatives.

Most Popular
003 / Combined

Combined (ECO + Alcantara)

Leather outer panels with fabric inserts — the best of both materials. Our most popular option. The contrast stitching and panel detail give a high-end finish you won't find off the shelf.
