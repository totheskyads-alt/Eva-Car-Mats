Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve your experience. They cannot harm your device or access other files on it.

2. How We Use Cookies

EVA Car Mats Ireland uses only essential and functional cookies. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party marketing cookies. The cookies we may use include:

  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used to keep the site functioning correctly during your visit.
  • Preference cookies — used to remember your cookie consent choice so you are not asked again on every visit.

3. What We Do Not Use

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any behavioural tracking tools
  • Facebook Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Any cookies that share your data with third parties

4. Your Choices

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a new cookie is set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

Useful links for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox — Options → Privacy & Security
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will be asked to accept or decline the use of cookies via a banner at the bottom of the page. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

6. GDPR Compliance

We are committed to complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive. We only use cookies that are strictly necessary or where you have given explicit consent.
